Replit instruction draft — Reminiscing rewrite

Rewrite the current “Reminiscing / Fragments of Before” sequence so it reflects the current Clinica story direction and reads as a smooth first-person narrative rather than short fragmented scene captions.

Story requirements

The reminiscence must clearly communicate:

The player originally lived in another world similar to Earth
They were a health student who wanted to help others but had not yet chosen a final healthcare path
The player’s possible future paths included nursing, medicine, public health, mental health, teaching, research, leadership, and other healing careers
Their first life ended unexpectedly
They awakened in Clinica, the fantasy world of healing
In that second life, they gained power too quickly and became overconfident
They mistook power for understanding
They failed against The Silent Infarction
Lotus Recall activated and sent them back for a second chance
The purpose of the second chance is not simply strength, but guided learning
Clinica University is the place where that true journey begins
Narrative tone

Write the sequence as if the player is remembering their own story.
Use connected first-person narrative prose, not short disconnected fragments.
The text should feel reflective, emotional, clear, and suspenseful.
Keep it mobile-readable with short paragraphs, but do not reduce it to one-line slogans.

Structure

Use 12–14 illustrated motion-comic scenes with donghua/manwha/anime-style visuals.

Recommended scene flow:

Before Clinica
A Future Not Yet Chosen
Why I Wanted to Heal
The Life I Thought Would Continue
The End of the First Life
Awakening in Clinica
The Power That Came Too Easily
Confidence Without Understanding
The Silent Infarction
The Failure
Lotus Recall
The Voice
Return to the Beginning
Clinica University
Important correction

The story should not frame the player only as a physician.
They should be presented as a health student with an undecided path, which better supports Clinica’s long-term healthcare career exploration theme.

Narrator

The mysterious guide should appear late in the reminiscence, near the final scenes.
Use The Lotus Keeper as the temporary identity.
The Lotus Keeper should feel ancient, calm, enigmatic, and instructional — not like a generic tooltip.

Example lines:

“You mistook power for mastery.”
“This time, you will understand before you act.”
“You were not returned to repeat your failure.”
“You were returned to learn.”
Art direction

Use the same illustrated art style already used in the better Clinica banners:

donghua/manwha/anime style
hand-drawn feel
clean character rendering
consistent fantasy-medical academy design language
warm academic scenes → dark failure scenes → white-gold recall → bright University hope

Avoid:

cyberpunk backgrounds
disconnected text fragments
generic stock fantasy
overly realistic hospital imagery
graphic death scenes
Motion-comic behavior

Do not make a full anime video.
Use:

full-screen illustrated panels
tap to continue
narration box at the bottom
subtle fade/zoom/light particle effects
optional skip after the first scene
visible progress indicator
final transition directly into Clinica University
Goal

The sequence should feel like a meaningful story chapter that explains why the player must study before entering the Ward, and it should emotionally support Clinica’s identity as a fantasy academy for healing, learning, and career discovery.
